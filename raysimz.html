Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services and pricing

What's included in your pricing?

Our pricing includes all development work, design, testing, and deployment. It also includes a specified period of post-launch support. Additional services like content creation, hosting, and ongoing maintenance can be added as needed.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes, we offer various support packages to meet your needs. Our basic plans include limited post-launch support, and we offer monthly retainer options for businesses that need ongoing maintenance and updates.

Can I make changes during development?

We understand that requirements can evolve. We build in flexibility for minor changes. Significant changes to scope may affect timeline and budget, which we'll discuss with you before proceeding.

What about hosting and domain?

We can recommend and set up hosting solutions that match your needs. Some clients prefer to handle this themselves, while others opt for our managed hosting services. We'll guide you through the options.

How do payments work?

We typically require a 30% deposit to begin work, with milestone payments throughout the project and a final payment upon completion. For larger projects, we can discuss alternative payment schedules.
